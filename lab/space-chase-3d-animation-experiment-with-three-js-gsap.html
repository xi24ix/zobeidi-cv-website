Space Chase - 3D Animation Experiment with Three.js & GSAP

Embracing the space

I’ve wanted to play around with some 3D web tech for a while and finally found a window. I present ‘Space Chase’ – An interactive 3D experiment with Three.js and GSAP. 🚀

Spaceship models courtesy of Poly.pizza with some additional textures added in Blender.

You can play with the interactive demo here: https://github.com/xi24ix/space-chase

https://youtu.be/\_ynF6RA6dIg

I’ve been pulling on the powers of Cursor AI to aid with this one, and it’s an incredible tool to have by your side for realising new concepts like this. Worth exploring if you haven’t already jumped on the Cursor train!
